Icebergs Everywhere

You sail for months and see a lot of icebergs. Food is running out and the crew is starting to get scurvy. A small building resembling an outhouse appears on the deck of your ship. A door opens and out comes a man wearing a large scarf. He says something about being the doctor and hands you a large bottle of pills that says Flintstones on it. He tells you to take these to cure your scurvy. He goes back into the little building and it disappears. The crew takes the pills and are healed the next day. You feel better now and think you see a clear path through the icebergs. You lost one crew member due to injury. You have @remainingcrew crew members remaining.
